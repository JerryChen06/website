Laboratory Techniques

Below, I have compiled the lab techniques in the life sciences I have learnt either through university or self-study. With each technique, I have linked it to a video recording of me walking through the technique in an introductory fashion (coloured green). For the nodes coloured grey, those are techniques I am either planning to learn, or currently preparing a video for. The purpose of this is to act as motivation for truly learning these techniques rather than just doing them once in class and moving on. My hope is that these videos will serve as useful review tools for myself (and hopefully others, eventually) down the line.
